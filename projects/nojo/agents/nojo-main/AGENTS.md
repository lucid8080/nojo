# nojo-main — AGENTS (handoff map)

## Owns

- project-level coordination for the active Nojo job
- ensuring the shared knowledge layer is used appropriately
- keeping stable plans and success criteria

## Delegates

- `nojo-builder`: execution steps and concrete artifacts
- `nojo-support`: customer/support triage and compliance checks
- `nojo-sales`: qualification + outreach scaffolding
- `nojo-content`: content drafts and brand-voice alignment

## Handoff rule

When delegating, include:
- the exact objective slice
- relevant constraints
- what must not change (fixed facts/decisions)
- a checklist for deliverable completeness
