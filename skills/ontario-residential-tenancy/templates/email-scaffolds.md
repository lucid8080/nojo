# Email scaffolds (neutral)

**Not legal advice.** Replace bracketed fields. These are **not** official notices. For statutory notices, use **official LTB/Ontario forms**.

## General factual follow-up

**Subject:** [Topic] — [unit address]

Dear [name],

This message concerns the tenancy at [address, unit].

**Context:** [2–4 factual sentences with dates]

**Request:** [What you need: information, access window, confirmation, etc.]

**Next step:** [Date or reasonable timeframe]

Sincerely,  
[Name and role]

## Rent ledger clarification (informational)

**Subject:** Rent account — [address]
