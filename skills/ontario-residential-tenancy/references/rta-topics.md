# RTA topic map (navigation)

High-level map of *Residential Tenancies Act, 2006* areas agents often need to look up on e-Laws or LTB guidance. **Not** a restatement of the law.

## Tenancy and agreement

- Application of the RTA; what is a rental unit; statutory exceptions (user must verify).
- Tenancy agreements; standard lease (where required); material terms; rules and additional terms.

## Rent

- Rent amount and changes; **rent increase** rules and notices (guidelines and exemptions where applicable).
- Rent deposits (last month’s rent); interest rules.

## Maintenance and services

- Landlord repair and maintenance obligations; tenant reporting; entry rules.
- Vital services (heat, water, etc.)—high urgency; factual and safety focus.

## Notices and termination

- Tenant’s and landlord’s notices; grounds; voiding and timing (fact-specific).
- Non-payment; persistent late payment; conduct; illegal acts; end of tenancy scenarios.

## Disputes and enforcement

- LTB applications in general terms; orders; compliance—user should verify procedure with official resources.

## Human rights and harassment

- Human rights in housing may overlap; avoid discriminatory language; serious issues may need specialist advice.

## Disclaimer

Use the official consolidation for precise wording. This map is for orientation only and is not legal advice.
