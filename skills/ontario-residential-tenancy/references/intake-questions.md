# Intake questions (expanded checklists)

Use the sections that match the user’s scenario. Skip irrelevant sections.

## All situations (baseline)

- Full rental address and legal unit description.
- Names of all tenants on the lease; occupants if relevant.
- Lease start; current term (periodic vs fixed); rent amount; payment frequency and due date.
- Who is communicating (landlord, property manager, tenant, guarantor)?
- Prior written notices or agreements about the same issue; LTB file numbers if any.

## Rent arrears (e.g. N4 path often discussed)

- Rent charged vs rent lawfully payable (any agreed changes documented?).
- Month-by-month shortfall or full months unpaid; partial payments applied how?
- Any repayment plan discussed; written or verbal?
- Last rent deposit (last month’s rent) amount and date taken.

## Conduct / damage / interference (e.g. N5 family often discussed)

- Specific incidents: dates, witnesses, evidence (photos, noise logs, police occurrence numbers if any).
- Warning letters or conversations; pattern vs single incident.
- Whether the issue implicates other tenants or safety.

## Maintenance

- What failed; when reported; how reported (email, portal, phone).
- Habitability or safety issues; urgent vs routine.
- Access attempts and landlord responses.

## End of tenancy / own use / renovation (examples)

- Stated reason for ending; move-in dates for landlord/purchaser if claimed.
- Compensation or notice periods per statute—**verify**; do not invent amounts or dates.

## After intake

- Flag missing facts before drafting.
- Recommend verification of **form version**, **service method**, and **timelines** on official sources.
